Our Story

B.C.A Group, Non-Profit Organization

Established in 2023, B.C.A Group is my response to the profound impact cancer has had on my life. Witnessing the struggles of my loved ones stirred a deep determination within me. Now, with more then 50 donations and counting, our charity stands as a source of hope for many.

Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness and provide vital resources for those navigating challenging situations.
